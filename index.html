Welcome to

JNR Globetrotters

JNR Globetrotters is one of the leading travel agencies of Gujarat. The main promoter, Uttej Rao is a veteran in the travel and tourism industry having been in this business since 1985 and worked with leading airlines like Panam, United Airlines, Royal Jordanian, Air Canada etc.

We are a One-stop travel shop providing all travel related services like International and domestic tickets, Outbound and Inbound Package Tours, Foreign Exchange, Visa assistance, Passport assistance, Hotels, Car rentals, Travel Insurance. We have successfully operated group and FIT package tours to various countries worldwide.

We have successfully operated many International group Packages and Incentive tours from Gujarat for some major Corporates.

Having carved a niche for ourselves in outbound travel we foraged in to inbound tourism and events particularly for Gujarat.
